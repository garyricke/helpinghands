About Us

Built on Heart,
Driven by Purpose

HelpingHands began with a simple but powerful idea: serve people who needed it most — whether elderly, too busy, or unable to afford expensive lawn and home care services.

Founded by four high school students, we built this business on the belief that hard work, reliability, and genuine care could set us apart. We pitched our story at the Incubator Entrepreneurship Pitch Competition — and won first place.

Today we're a full-service operation serving Batavia and the Tri-Cities area, one yard, one home, one helping hand at a time.
